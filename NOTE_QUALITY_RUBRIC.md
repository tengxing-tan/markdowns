# NOTE QUALITY RUBRIC

Use this to score each note quickly and decide whether to keep, archive, merge, or remove.

## Score (1 to 5 each)

1. Retrieval Trigger

- 1: I do not know when I would open this.
- 3: I might open this for a specific task.
- 5: I know exactly when to open this.

2. Reuse Horizon

- 1: One-time note, unlikely to reuse.
- 3: Might help occasionally.
- 5: Reusable across many projects or years.

3. Actionability

- 1: Mostly thoughts, no clear action.
- 3: Some actionable parts.
- 5: Clear steps, checklist, commands, or decisions.

4. Signal Density

- 1: Mostly filler or broad statements.
- 3: Mixed value.
- 5: High-value details, examples, edge cases.

5. Maintenance Cost

- 1: Becomes stale quickly and needs frequent updates.
- 3: Occasional updates needed.
- 5: Mostly evergreen with low maintenance.

## Total Score

Total = sum of all 5 criteria (min 5, max 25)

## Decision Rules

- 21 to 25: KEEP_CORE
- 16 to 20: KEEP_ARCHIVE
- 11 to 15: MERGE (or rewrite into a stronger parent note)
- 5 to 10: REMOVE_CANDIDATE

## Tie-Breakers

Use these if a note is borderline:

1. Compliance or production risk content wins (keep).
2. Real postmortem with root cause and prevention wins (keep).
3. Duplicate notes lose (merge/remove).
4. Trend-only or personal-journal content loses (remove from technical vault).

## One-Line Test

If future-me cannot answer both questions in 10 seconds, downgrade the note:

- When will I open this?
- What decision or action will this help me make?
